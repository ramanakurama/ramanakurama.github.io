Dr Kurama Venkata Ramana is a Professor of Computer Science Engineering at JNTUK where he currently is positioned as Officer on Special Duty to the Vice Chancellor at JNTUK. He specialises in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. He explores new methods for improving computer vision models for Medical Imaging.

As Officer on Special Duty at Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada his role not only involves teaching but also to chair many selection committee meetings and to advise on policies, academic, administrative, financial, and legal matters of the university and its affiliated colleges.

Professor Ramana Kurama also advises technology companies that are developing media technologies and services with the aim of improving the Medical Diagnosis Research and Machine Learning in Scale.

An author of various academic papers published in popular journals and conferences, his significant work on Neural Network based classification and detection of Brain Haemorrhages helped many doctors perceive knowledge on Machine Learning and Computer Science. A native of India, K V Ramana received a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics Communication and Engineering from the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Master of Technology in Computer Science from University of Hyderabad and Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Science from Rayalaseema University.
